About Cardinal Pilates

At Cardinal Pilates, we believe every body is capable of transformation. Led by Katherine, a Polestar Pilates-trained instructor and former attorney, our studio creates a sanctuary where movement meets joy.

We focus on functional, mindful movement designed to improve your posture, flexibility, and overall strength. Whether you're a beginner discovering Pilates for the first time or an advanced practitioner, our personalized approach ensures you feel supported every step of the way.

With 5 Balanced Body studio reformers, multiple fitness props, and an intimate setting, you'll leave feeling stronger, more aligned, and ready to take on your week.
