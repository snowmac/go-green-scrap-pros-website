Why can't I throw my TV in the trash?

TVs contain lead, mercury, and cadmium that contaminate soil and groundwater in landfills. Colorado law requires proper e-waste disposal. We ensure your TV goes to a certified recycling processor.

Do you pick up CRT (tube) TVs?

Yes. We pick up all types — CRT, LED, LCD, plasma, and projection TVs. CRTs are especially important to recycle properly due to the lead in the glass funnel.

Can you pick up computer monitors too?

Yes. Computer monitors are e-waste and need the same proper recycling as TVs. We can bundle monitors in the same pickup as your TV.
