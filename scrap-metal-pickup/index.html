Why Free for Some Loads? How Does This Work?

Scrap metal has real market value. Steel, aluminum, copper, and brass are bought by recycling facilities at per-pound rates. For high-value loads — commercial HVAC units, server racks, clean copper or brass batches — the recycling value covers the cost of my time and fuel, so pickup is free. For everything else, the load's recycling value doesn't fully cover the trip, so a flat dispatch fee applies based on distance. Either way, the metal gets recycled instead of dumped.

I'm genuinely passionate about metal recycling — it's the part of this business I'd do even if there weren't a financial incentive. Keeping good metal out of landfills matters.

Need the Metal Moved From Inside?

The dispatch fee (or free-for-qualifying-loads rate) covers curbside or easily accessible metal. If you need help moving heavy metal items from inside your home, garage, or basement to the curb — or if you'd like us to handle a full metal-heavy cleanout — inside pickup starts at an additional $100 for the labor.
