Why Free? How Does This Work?

Scrap metal has real market value. Steel, aluminum, copper, and brass are bought by recycling facilities at per-pound rates. When I pick up your metal, sort it, and deliver it to a recycler, the sale covers the cost of my time and fuel. You get free pickup. The metal gets recycled instead of dumped. Everybody wins.

I'm genuinely passionate about metal recycling — it's the part of this business I'd do even if there weren't a financial incentive. Keeping good metal out of landfills matters.
