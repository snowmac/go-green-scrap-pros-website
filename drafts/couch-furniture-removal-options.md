Getting rid of a couch sounds simple until you're staring at a sectional that won't fit through the doorway. Furniture removal is one of the most common requests we get across the Denver metro, and the right approach depends on what you've got, what condition it's in, and how fast you need it gone.

Here's a straight breakdown of your three main options — and how to decide which fits your situation.

## Option 1: Donate It

Donation is the best outcome for furniture that still has life in it. A clean, intact couch or chair can go to someone who needs it, stay out of the landfill, and earn you a tax deduction if you itemize.

### When donation works

- The piece is structurally sound with no broken frames or legs
- Fabric is free of major stains, tears, pet hair buildup, or odors
- You can get it to a drop-off location or schedule a pickup

### Denver-area donation options for furniture

- **ARC Thrift Stores** — Colorado-based organization that offers free furniture pickup along the Front Range. Items must be in good, immediately reusable condition. Schedule through their website.
- **Goodwill of Colorado** — Accepts furniture at select locations. Call ahead to confirm current acceptance.
- **Habitat for Humanity ReStore** — Takes furniture, particularly solid wood pieces and items in good structural shape.
- **Salvation Army** — Offers scheduled furniture pickup. Wait times vary by season.
- **Facebook Marketplace / Buy Nothing Groups** — For a free listing, local neighbors often claim furniture within hours.

### The honest catch

Most donation organizations will decline furniture that's heavily worn, stained, or smells like pets or smoke. If you're not sure, call ahead — or be prepared with a backup plan.

## Option 2: Recycle the Materials

Standard upholstered furniture — a couch with foam, fabric, and a wood or metal frame — isn't a typical curbside recyclable. But the components often have recycling value:

- **Metal frames and hardware** can be separated and taken to a scrap metal facility. Steel sofa frames and metal chair legs have real value.
- **Wood frames** from solid-wood furniture may be accepted at wood recycling facilities.
- **Foam and fabric** are generally the hardest components to divert from landfill, though some textile recyclers in Colorado accept upholstery material.

When Go Green Scrap Pros picks up furniture, we separate recyclable metal and wood from material that genuinely needs to be disposed of. You're not just getting a haul — you're getting responsible sorting.

## Option 3: Haul It Away

Sometimes a couch just needs to go. Heavy wear, pest concerns, water damage, or simply no local takers — not every piece of furniture can be donated or sold, and that's okay.

### DIY haul-away

If you have a truck and a helper, you can haul furniture to the Denver Arapahoe Disposal Site (DADS) or other transfer stations serving Adams, Jefferson, and Boulder counties. Most charge by weight or by load. Check current hours and fees before heading out — they vary by location and change seasonally.

The real challenge with DIY is usually the move itself: getting a couch down stairs, through tight hallways, and into a truck bed without wrecking your back or your walls.

## What About Curbside?

Denver and surrounding municipalities have varying rules on large item curbside pickup. Some cities offer bulky item collection days — check with your city's waste management department for your specific address. Be aware that leaving furniture at the curb without scheduling a pickup may violate local ordinances in some communities and can result in fines.

Also: furniture left at the curb overnight in Denver neighborhoods often disappears by morning — which is fine if it's in good condition and you don't mind the informal donation. Less fine if it sits there for days.

## Making the Call

A quick decision guide:

- **Clean, intact, no odors?** → Try donation first, then Facebook Marketplace
- **Good bones, rough fabric?** → Consider scrap for the frame, haul the rest
- **Damaged, stained, heavy?** → Call for a professional pickup
