The Guy Behind the Trailer

I started this business because I saw how overpriced and impersonal junk removal had become in the Denver area. The big franchise services charge a premium, upsell at the door, and treat your stuff like it's all headed straight to the landfill. I also genuinely love scrap metal. It's one of the most satisfying parts of this job — identifying what's in a pile, sorting the copper from the aluminum from the steel, and knowing it's going to a recycler instead of a dump. That enthusiasm carries through everything we do.

Why "Go Green"?

The name reflects a real commitment. Every job, we ask: what can be recycled? What can be donated? What actually needs to be thrown away? Most junk removal companies skip that question and dump everything. We don't.

Scrap metal never goes to the landfill — it goes to a recycler. Mattresses go to a recycling facility where the steel springs, foam, and fabric are separated and reused. Furniture in good condition gets offered to donation centers before we load it for disposal. Electronics go to certified e-waste recyclers.

That's what "green" means to us. Not a buzzword — an actual habit we practice on every job.
